Our Reading Clubs

Grace and Smiles currently runs four reading clubs in Ghana. Namely the Palladium Square community reading club, Osu Children’s Home, Kanda Cluster of schools reading club and a club at the Royal Seed Orphanage, Kasoa, Ghana. It is our hope that our reading clubs will benefit the many children in these areas whom due to poverty have not been able to develop the interest and love of reading.We have also donated books and stationery to local schools, community kids and orphanages in Ghana.

About Us

The Foundation

The Grace and Smiles Foundation is a UK Registered Charity. Founded in 2016, it was set up to meet a specific need in deprived communities in Ghana, West Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to address poverty among families in rural communities by providing donated children’s clothing, shoes, toys and books to these underprivileged families. We also hope to contribute towards education development by holding regular reading clubs to improve reading and literacy skills among the children in these communities.
